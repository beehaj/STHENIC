ABOUT US

Sthenic Finance and Advisory Limited (“Sthenic”) is a structured finance and investment company with special focus on supporting growth business at resolving short and medium term liquidity challenges through innovative financing products and structures.
Our strategy is built on the strength, agility and speed of our execution. We bring together entrenched investors seeking alpha and growth companies seeking investment that unlock high returns to create increased value.
The Sthenic approach is digging deep at solutions regardless of the profile of the deal be it special situation investing, financing, advisory and in growth situation.

History

Founded in 2016, Sthenic is built on a solution mindset aimed at unlocking corporate values through the resolution of financial and liquidity related challenges of Nigerian mid tier and small companies in their growth phase.
Our thesis is that more than 45% of Nigerian companies are seeking funding to sustain business growth, fund opportunistic transactions, or even keep good businesses afloat. Sthenic solutions include speed, structural flexibility, and uniqueness to purpose.
We also recognize the weaknesses of corporate structures in many growing Nigerian companies. What we do best is providing a mix of active and passive handholding, and risk management structure, in a recursive learning mode, without disrupting the operational comfort of our client.
